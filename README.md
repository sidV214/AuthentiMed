> **"Building a world where 'Authenticity Guaranteed' is a mathematical certainty, not a marketing promise."**

AuthentiMed is a dual-layered security ecosystem designed to eradicate counterfeit medications from the global supply chain. By integrating **Deep Learning Computer Vision** with **Immutable Blockchain Ledgers**, we provide an end-to-end verification framework from the factory floor to the patient's hand.

---

## üöÄ The Problem
* **Human Cost**: Counterfeit drugs contribute to over **1 million deaths** annually.
* **Economic Impact**: The pharmaceutical industry loses over **$200 billion** per year to illicit trade.
* **Technical Gaps**: Existing QR codes are easily cloned, and centralized databases remain a "single point of failure".

## üí° Our Solution: The "Dual-Shield"
We implement a two-factor verification process to ensure total drug integrity:
1. **AI Physical Fingerprinting**: Analyzes microscopic packaging anomalies and OCR labels that are impossible to replicate manually.
2. **Blockchain Digital Twin**: Records every transaction on a decentralized ledger, ensuring the digital record matches the physical product.
